The Final Frontier

Space is not just a place above us. It is a vast, mysterious expanse filled with billions of galaxies, each containing billions of stars. Humanity has only scratched the surface of what lies beyond our atmosphere.

Our Solar System

The eight planets of our solar system each have their own unique characteristics. Click on any planet card to learn more!

Mars

Mars is called the Red Planet. It has the tallest volcano in the solar system - Olympus Mons, standing at 72,000 feet!

Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet. Its Great Red Spot is a storm that has been raging for over 350 years!

Saturn

Saturn's rings are made of ice and rock. The planet is so light it could float in water if there was a bathtub big enough!

Earth

Earth is the only planet known to support life. It is 4.5 billion years old and 71% of its surface is covered in water.

Famous Space Missions

Apollo 11

Year: 1969

The first mission to land humans on the Moon. Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin walked on the lunar surface while Michael Collins orbited above.

Voyager 1

Year: 1977

Launched in 1977, Voyager 1 is now the farthest human-made object from Earth. It carries a golden record with sounds and images of life on Earth.

Mars Perseverance

Year: 2020

NASA's rover is exploring Mars, searching for signs of ancient life and collecting rock samples for future return to Earth.
